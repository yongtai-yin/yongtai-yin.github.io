### Brief Introduction

This ongoing research addresses common issues in current time-modulated antenna array (TMAA) systems for DOA estimation, including colored noise, high computational complexity, and sampling synchronization errors. By developing improved algorithms, we aim to respond to these challenges and enhance estimation accuracy in practical applications.
