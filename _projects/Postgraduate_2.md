### Brief Introduction

Our study aims to advance channel estimation for massive MIMO systems in IoT applications. We propose a parameterized multipath channel estimation method, in the presence of array gain-phase perturbation. Our method demonstrates superior accuracy, promising improved signal transmission efficiency and reliability.
