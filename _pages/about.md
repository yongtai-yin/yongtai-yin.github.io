Hey, thanks for stopping by! 👋

Here is Yongtai Yin (Aaron, 殷永泰).

I'm a *Master's student* at Northwestern Polytechnical University, specializing in *Information and Communication Engineering*, co-advised by Prof. [Ling Wang](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/author/37578965000) and Assoc. Prof. [Yuexian Wang](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-3622-6162). Prior to this, I earned my *Bachelor's degree* in *Communication Engineering* from the same institution. My passion lies in `Signal Processing` and `Wireless Communication`, including DOA estimation, source localization and tracking, massive MIMO, and channel estimation.

**I'm excited to share that I have set my sights on pursuing a PhD in the fall of 2024.** I'm committed to diving deeper into research and innovation in the fields I'm passionate about. My journey has been marked by impactful research experiences and publications, which have fueled my desire to contribute more to the advancement of knowledge in these areas.

**If you're interested in connecting, discussing research, or learning more about my journey, feel free to reach out.**
