### Brief Introduction

Introducing an auxiliary vehicle positioning system with time-modulated antenna arrays, our research enhances localization accuracy in occluded scenes. Leveraging multiple auxiliary sources and employing Doppler shifts in received signals, we achieve superior direct vehicle localization accuracy, outperforming existing methods.
