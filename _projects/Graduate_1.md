### Brief Introduction

Addressing mutual coupling effects in antenna arrays, we applied interference-plus-noise covariance matrix reconstruction to achieve robust beamforming. This approach accommodates angle-independent and angle-dependent scenarios respectively, ensuring enhanced performance under mutual coupling mismatch.
