## About Me

I am currently a research assistant in the Department of Electronic Engineering at [The Chinese University of Hong Kong](https://www.cuhk.edu.hk), supervised by [Prof. Wing-Kin Ma](https://www.ee.cuhk.edu.hk/~wkma/). Before that, I obtained my Master's Degree in Information and Communication Engineering from [Northwestern Polytechnical University](https://en.nwpu.edu.cn/), supervised by Prof. Ling Wang and Prof. Yuexian Wang. I also received my Bachelor’s Degree in Communication Engineering from [Northwestern Polytechnical University](https://en.nwpu.edu.cn/).

## Research Interests

- **Signal Processing:** DOA estimation, source localization and tracking
- **Wireless Communication:** massive MIMO, Channel estimation, near-field communication
